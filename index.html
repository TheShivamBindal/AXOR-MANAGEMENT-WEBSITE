About Us

FleX Management Consulting (FMC) was founded in 2022 by Dr. Ed Dandalt in Toronto, Canada, while he was serving as a Professor of Leadership at Wilfrid Laurier University. Dr. Dandalt holds a PhD from McGill University, an MA from the University of Toronto, and an MSc from Universite Paris 1 Pantheon-Sorbonne.

FMC offers a unique, cost-effective alternative in a market where large management consulting firms often charge higher fees for public policy and organizational advisory services. Our clients benefit from high-quality, research-informed expertise delivered by experienced scholars, doctoral-level professionals, and specialized consultants.

Who We Are

FMC is a public management research and coaching firm specializing in the design of strategic management systems, evidence-based and data-driven business solutions, and public policy development.

We provide leadership development and training services to:

✓ Public sector organizations (e.g., government departments, municipalities)

✓ Third sector agencies (e.g., NGOs, non-profits)

✓ Private sector firms engaged in corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives
