About Us

AXOR Management Consulting was founded in 2022 by Dr. Ed Dandalt in Toronto. Dr. Dandalt holds a PhD from McGill University and is a Full-time University Professor in Canada. He is a public management scholar with publications in management, public administration, and leadership journals.

He has presented research papers on AI, leadership, technology, public governance, and crisis management at international academic conferences in Germany, the United Kingdom, Brazil, Canada and the United States.

Who We Are

AXOR is a public management research and coaching firm specializing in the design of strategic management systems, evidence-based and data-driven business solutions, and public policy development.

We provide leadership development and training services to:

• Third sector agencies (e.g., NGOs, non-profits)

• Public sector organizations (e.g., government departments)

• Private sector firms engaged in CSR initiatives

What We Do

Our policy advisory services help local and national governments, international organizations, and non-profits effectively address complex managerial, bureaucratic, societal, community, business and organizational needs and challenges.
